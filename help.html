How to Order

Follow these simple steps to place your order successfully.

Step 1: Browse the Menu

Visit the Menu Page to explore our delicious offerings.

Step 2: Select Your Items

Click on an item to view its details and then click "Add to Cart" to include it in your order.

Step 3: Review Your Cart

Click on the cart icon to view your selected items. You can adjust quantities or remove items before proceeding.

Step 4: Proceed to Checkout

Once you're happy with your selection, click "Order Now" to proceed to the checkout page.

Step 5: Enter Your Details

Fill in your name, contact information, and delivery details in the order form.

Step 6: Confirm and Place Order

Review your order summary and click "Place Order" to finalize your purchase.
